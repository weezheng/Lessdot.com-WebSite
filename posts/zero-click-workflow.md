# Precision for the Soul

As a data scientist, I spend my life optimizing flows. But why do we only apply this to manufacturing? 

In this post, I share my journey on how I use smart logic to simplify my daily life, filter the noise, and focus on what truly matters: human connection and real experience.

## The Strategy

1. **Information Triage**: Automating my inbox with logic-based tagging.
2. **Mental Bandwidth**: Reducing choices through systemic routine.
3. **Presence**: Using tech to disconnect when it's time to live.
